Base Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Base collects no data. Period.

Data Collection

Base does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information. The app has no analytics SDK, no crash reporter, no advertising framework, and no account system.

Local Storage

Calculator history and unit converter preferences are stored exclusively on your device using UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time from within the app.

Network

Base makes no network requests. Currency conversion rates are bundled with the app and are approximate.

Third-Party Services

Base uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the update date at the top will reflect it. No material change will reduce your privacy.

Contact

Questions: uvilla11@users.noreply.github.com
