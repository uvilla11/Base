# Base

## What it is
A personal number & unit Swiss-army-knife for iOS. Calculator, unit converter, and tip/split calculator in one clean, single-screen app. No ads, no permissions, no account, no subscription.

## Who it's for
Everyday iPhone users frustrated by ad-ridden converters and the decade-stale Calculator.app. People who want quick math AND unit conversion without switching apps.

## Register
Product — design serves the tool. Fast, glanceable, thumb-driven. The app must feel native, premium, and silent-satisfying (haptics over sound).

## Core jobs
1. Quick arithmetic with visible expression and history
2. Convert length / mass / volume / temperature / currency instantly
3. Split a bill with tip in under 10 seconds

## Non-goals
Scientific calculator (V2), custom unit presets (V2), widgets (V2), cloud sync, accounts, analytics.

## Monetization
Free + $2.99 tip-jar. Zero dark patterns.

## Quality bar
<50ms launch, zero third-party dependencies, one-handed use, works offline, App Store 4.7+ polish.
